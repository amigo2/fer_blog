Curious animal.

I mainly consider myself a curious person and definitively a beauty observer. Since always, I've loved to draw and started my career as a digital artist back in Spain. At present, I'm looking for a job as a Django developer mainly with a point of view in ML, AI, and bioinformatics.

It took a long time till I started to do some serious coding, everything started with 3Ds max. As a digital artist and amateur coder, it was inevitable, sooner or later I will create my tools and plugins. I started creating some plugging and bones systems in max scripts... Later on, I fully immersed and decide to study computer science to understand what was going on. It is just not along with that I transformed myself from games programmer and University lecturer to web programming and science like Machine Learning and bioinformatics.
